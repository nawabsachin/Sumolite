FAQs


What is Sumolite footwear made of?
Sumolite footwear is crafted using a proprietary blend of lightweight and durable materials,
including high-grade polymers and synthetic fabrics. These materials are chosen to provide
optimal comfort, flexibility, and support for everyday wear.
How should I clean my Sumolite slipper?
To clean your Sumolite slipper, simply wipe them with a damp cloth or sponge using mild
soap and water. Avoid using harsh chemicals or abrasive cleaning agents, as they may damage the
materials. Allow your shoes to air dry naturally, away from direct heat sources
Can I customize my Sumolite slippers?
Sumolite slippers are designed to provide comfort and style straight out of the box. While
customization options may be limited, you can personalize your slippers with accessories like
decorative pins or charms to add a personal touch.
Can I return or exchange Sumolite slipper if they don't fit?
Yes, Sumolite typically allows returns or exchanges within a specified timeframe, provided that
the shoes are unworn, in their original condition, and accompanied by proof of purchase. Be sure
to review Sumolite's return and exchange policy for details on eligibility criteria and procedures.
